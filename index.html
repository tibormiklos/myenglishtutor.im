One-to-one English literature and language tuition

What I do

I provide one-to-one English literature and language tuition from my home in Laxey to pupils across the Island. I teach adult learners, GCSE students and primary pupils. My hours are flexible – get in touch to find a slot that suits you.

The Teaching

I provide exam-specific guidance and practise; I also help with basic skills: sentence structure, punctuation and reading comprehension.

I like to tailor my lessons to the interests of my pupils, and I encourage my pupils, as they progress, to become increasingly involved in the planning – this way they learn to take ownership of the process.

Supporting Parents

It can be frustrating to feel that you are cut off from what your child is learning at school.

I am always available to talk personally to you about your child’s progress and provide you with support and materials if you would like to augment their learning at home.

Pricing and payment

I charge £30 per hour with no extra charges for materials or meetings. You can pay monthly or termly in advance by cheque or bank transfer. We’ll start with a free, no-commitment initial consultation to find out how I can help you and your child.

Copywriting

I write blog articles and content on the subjects of design, technology and the environment.

Get in touch to talk about what I can do for you.

About me

I am an English graduate of Edinburgh University with a PGCE from Brunel. I have been providing private tuition to pupils from Year 5 to Year 11 for over 5 years.

What I love about this work is that it gives me the opportunity to watch my pupils grow in confidence and ability over the years.

There is nothing more satisfying than the expression on someone’s face when they produce a piece of work that they are proud of – or when they overcome an obstacle that they thought they would never conquer.

This is why I do it.
